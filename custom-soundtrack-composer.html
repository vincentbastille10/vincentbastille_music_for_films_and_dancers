Custom Soundtrack Composer for Dance & Film

Beyond existing releases, Vincent Bastille works as a custom soundtrack composer for contemporary dance, short films, installations and hybrid projects. The goal is simple: find the exact sound that your project needs, without overcomplicating the process.

For whom?

  • Choreographers and dance schools building a new piece.
  • Directors and editors working on short or medium-length films.
  • Visual artists preparing installations, performances or gallery work.
  • Companies producing brand films with a more artistic, cinematic tone.

Musical style & influences

Vincent’s sound combines:

  • Ambient and atmospheric textures for space and depth.
  • Minimal piano inspired by composers like Erik Satie.
  • Trip-hop and downtempo shadows in the spirit of Portishead and related scenes.
  • Liquid drum & bass for movement, flow and emotional release.
  • Sound design and field recordings when the project calls for it.

A simple, human process

A typical collaboration follows a few clear steps:

  1. First exchange — you share a short description, references and what you expect from the music.
  2. Moodboard & direction — together you refine the sound universe (textures, tempo, instrumentation).
  3. First demos — Vincent sends a few cues or themes to validate the direction.
  4. Adjustments — tempo, structure, density, intensity are adjusted to fit choreography or editing.
  5. Final masters — clean files are delivered in the formats you need (WAV, stems if necessary).

What you can expect

  • Clear and honest communication about what is possible in your timeline.
  • A real dialogue between image, movement and sound.
  • Music that supports the project instead of taking over.
  • Flexibility to adapt to low-budget or independent contexts when needed.

Contact & project details

To start a conversation, you can send:

  • a short description of your project,
  • links to rehearsals, a teaser or stills, if available,
  • deadlines and approximate duration,
  • your context (school, company, independent, brand, etc.).
